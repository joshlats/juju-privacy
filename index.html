Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Juju du Jour does not collect your personal information. Everything you enter — names, dates, your practice — stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Juju du Jour has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no servers that store your information. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

What stays on your device

The app saves the following locally on your phone, so that your practice is there when you return:

  • The name or nickname you choose for your child, and your own name, if you enter them
  • An optional birthday or due date, if you enter one
  • Which blessings you have received, and on which days
  • Your reminder time and app preferences
  • Anonymous counts of app activity (for example, how many blessings have been opened)

This information never leaves your device unless you choose to share it. Deleting the app deletes this information.

Notifications

If you turn on a daily reminder, it is scheduled locally by your phone. No notification service, server, or third party is involved, and no information about you is sent anywhere.

Sharing a blessing

When you choose to share a blessing, your phone's standard share sheet sends only what you see: the artwork and the mantra. Your child's name is not included unless you add it. What happens after that is governed by whichever app you share through — Messages, Mail, or another service.

Purchasing a print

If you tap to purchase a print, you leave Juju du Jour and open an external website in your browser. Any information you provide there is handled by that site under its own privacy policy, not ours.

Analytics and advertising

There are none. Juju du Jour contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, no tracking software, and no advertising identifiers. We do not build profiles of our users, because we do not have any information with which to build them.

Children

Juju du Jour is designed for adults — parents, caregivers, and family members. Although the app is about a child, it is not directed to children, and it does not knowingly collect information from anyone. Any information a parent enters about their child remains on that parent's device.

Your control

Because your information lives only on your phone, you control it completely. You can edit or clear what you have entered at any time in the app's Settings, and deleting the app removes everything. There is no account to close and no data for us to delete on your behalf, because we never had it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if we ever add optional cloud backup — we will update this page and note the date above. Any feature that would involve storing your information outside your device would be optional and require your clear consent.

Contact

Questions about this policy are welcome at hello@jujudujour.com.
